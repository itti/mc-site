ਹਤਹਿਂ ਨਿਸਾਨੇ ਪਰ ਮਿਲਹਿਂ ਕੈ ਅਖੇਰ ਕੇ ਸਾਥ ।
Wearing a sword around His neck, and a gun in His hand, *Guru Gobind Singh Ji* would spend His time engaged in target practice or hunting.
